At every Linaro Connect we end the week on a high note with our Demo Friday Technology Showcase. Demo Friday will be held on Friday 21 September 2018 from 12pm-2pm.

If you are interested in presenting a demo on Demo Friday, please complete the application. There are a limited number of slots so it will be a case of first come first served! If you have any questions or would like to express an interest in showcasing a demo, click on the button below or email connect@linaro.org

Please note: We are using a new system to collect all proposals and demos for YVR18 and you will be required to enter your email address and create a password in order to submit your demo application. You will then be able to login and modify your submission, check on the status, and receive updates and instructions.
